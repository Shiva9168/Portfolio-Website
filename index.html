Project Echo
Project Echo is an upcoming game set in a post-apocalyptic world, where humanity struggles to survive after a catastrophic event that nearly wiped out all life on earth. The game is a single-player, story-driven action-adventure that takes players on a journey through a devastated landscape, filled with danger and uncertainty. Players take on the role of Aleia, a young woman who must navigate this dangerous world to uncover the secrets of her past and uncover the truth about what happened to the world. The game features a dynamic combat system, where players can use a variety of weapons and abilities to fight against a range of enemies, from mutated creatures to rogue factions of survivors. While Project Echo is currently in development under the code name, it promises to be a thrilling and immersive experience for players, with a rich story, captivating characters, and a beautiful world to explore.

Gas Station Clicker
Gas Station Clicker was a Discord bot that we created in collaboration with another developer. The bot allowed users to manage a virtual gas station, buy fuel, fill cars, and earn money. It was an idle tycoon type game that was easy to plug into any Discord server without configuration. Unfortunately, due to our studies and other commitments, we had to shut down the bot. We are grateful for the support we received from the community during its lifetime. It was used in over 2000 servers and brought hours of fun and entertainment to Discord users.
